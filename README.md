# MAUVE-SUPERSTORE-SALES-AND-CUSTOMER-SATISFACTION-ANALYSIS


**Author:** Ademijube Victoria Olamide

**Date:** 2025-12-09

 --- 

## Problem Statement 
- Bank managers are complaining of understaff and overtime cost.
- Uncertainty around whether the Membership Program drives meaningful revenue.
- Marketing efforts appear misaligned with actual customer buying patterns.
- There is concerns about Customer satisfaction of high-value shoppers.


 --- 

## Project Objective 
- Identify true peak transaction hours for each branch.
- Compare spending behaviour between Members and Normal customers.
- Analyse buyer demographics for key product categories to improve marketing efficiency.
- Explore the link between customer spending and satisfaction levels.


--- 

## Dashboard Features
- Total sales : 21000
- Total quantity sold : 115541 
- Total revenue : ₦ 635M 
- Average rating: 7.0 (there is still need for improvement)
 
--- 

## Key Findings 
- The quantity of goods sold at each hour in the three branches are similar, ranging from 550-640.
- Peak activity occurs in the evening, where all cities show a sharp rise in transactions.
- Chicago leads in the morning and late evening, while Austin peaks at 17.00.
- Female customers recorded higher sales, though with little difference, in home & life style, fashion accessories and sport and travel.
- The males are more active buyers of electronic accessories and food & beverages.
- New York branch consistently performs well across categories, especially in Food & Beverages, and Electronic Accessories.
- Chicago store excels in Home & Lifestyle and Fashion Accessories.
- Austin branch performs best in Sports & Travel.
- Members and Non-members generate almost the same revenue per transaction.
- Members buy slightly more quantity
- The membership program does not significantly outperform normal customers on transaction value.
- There is no significant difference of customer satisfaction among difference level.
- Premium customers are not more dissatisfied; they rate the store the same as everyone else








--- 

## Recommendations 
- Employ more staffs and increase the number at all working hours especially 16:00–19:00 across all branches.
- Redesign the loyalty program to reward frequency and drive larger basket sizes.
- Launch male-focused campaigns in underperforming segments to balance revenue contribution.
- Enhance general service quality to improve customer satisfaction.
